![](https://giki.edu.pk/rd/rd-fcs/wre-2021/)![](https://giki.edu.pk/rd/rd-fcs/wre-2021/)GIK Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology is organizing the 4th International Workshop on Functional Reverse Engineering of Machine Tools (WRE 2022) on 17th December, 2022. The Workshop has the technical sponsorship of IMechE (UK) and IEEE (USA), and the financial sponsorship of the Directorate General of Science and Technology, Government of KP. WRE 2022 invites original book chapters to be published by CRC Press (Taylor & Francis Group, USA). The book chapters of [WRE 2018](https://giki.edu.pk/rd/rd-fcs/wre2018/) and [WRE 2020](https://giki.edu.pk/rd/rd-fcs/wre2020/) have already been published by CRC Press () (
[![](https://giki.edu.pk/rd/rd-fcs/wre-2021/)](https://giki.edu.pk/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/WRE-2018-Program.pdf)
**[WRE 2022 Program is available here](https://giki.edu.pk/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/WRE-2022-PROGRAM-Final-.pdf).**
**WRE 2022 Registration is now close.**
WRE 2022 invites book chapters on the chosen topics listed [here](https://giki.edu.pk/rd/rd-fcs/wre2020/call-for-book-chapters/). Book chapters accepted after a double-blind review process will be published by CRC Press (Taylor & Francis Group, USA) as a book series (Computer in Engineering Design and Manufacturing Series).
