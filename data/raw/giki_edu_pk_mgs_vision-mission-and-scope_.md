## School Vision
To be amongst the top business schools in the country and play a leadership role in the field of management sciences education.
## School Mission
The School of Management Sciences is highly committed to:
  1. Educate and develop such business graduates who create value for their stakeholders and society at large.
  2. Build such a conducive environment for faculty and students which is diverse and inclusive, led by learning, entrepreneurial, and research orientation.
