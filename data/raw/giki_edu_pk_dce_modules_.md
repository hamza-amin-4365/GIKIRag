Civil engineering is a diverse field where many theoretical and practical courses are integrated to deliver knowledge related to built environment, infrastructure development & management. The courses we offer cover:
  * Mathematics
  * Soil Mechanics
  * Structures
  * Hydraulics
  * Environmental Engineering
  * Fluid Mechanics
  * Engineering Surveying & Geology
  * Construction Engineering & Management
  * Building Information Modelling (BIM) and Design
