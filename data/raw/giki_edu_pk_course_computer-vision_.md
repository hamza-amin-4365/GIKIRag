**Computer Vision** | **Hours: 3 1 4**  
---|---  
Foundation of image formation, measurement, and analysis, geometric relationships between 2D images and the 3D world, object and scene recognition and categorization from images, fundamentals of image formation, camera imaging geometry, image filtering, feature detection, and matching, stereo vision, motion estimation, and tracking, image classification, and scene understanding.
